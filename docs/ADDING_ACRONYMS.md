# Adding Acronyms

The permanent built-in glossary lives in:

```text
data/acronyms.js
```

Each acronym entry follows this shape:

```js
{
  acronym: "HVAC",
  term: "Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning",
  category: "Building Services",
  short: "Systems used to heat, cool, ventilate, and manage indoor air quality.",
  plainEnglish: "HVAC covers the equipment and controls used to manage the air and temperature inside a building or space.",
  stageContext: "You may hear HVAC mentioned around venues, offices, warehouses, equipment rooms, control rooms, plant areas, or installation projects.",
  example: "A dimmer room, rack room, or control space may need suitable HVAC so that equipment and people do not overheat.",
  remember: "HVAC = controls the air and temperature in a building."
}
```

## Recommended wording style

Keep each entry:

- Plain English
- Short enough to learn quickly
- Public-safe
- Generic, not project-specific
- Honest when something needs confirmation

## Good categories to use

- Electrical Safety
- Circuit Protection
- Testing & Compliance
- Lighting Control
- Lighting Equipment
- Lifting & Rigging
- Power Distribution
- Health & Safety
- Building Services
- Standards & Organisations
- To Confirm

## Important public-safe rule

Do not add customer names, internal documents, pricing, job details, project names, drawings, login details or confidential Stage Electrics information.
